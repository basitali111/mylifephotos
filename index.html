Meri Pyaari Fiza Basit,

Aaj tumhare birthday pe main tumhe yeh batana chahta hoon ke tum meri zindagi ki sabse khoobsurat cheez ho. Jab se tum meri life mein aayi ho, sab kuch badal gaya hai. Tum meri dhoop ho, meri barish ho, meri khushi ho aur mera sukoon ho. 💖

Tumhari muskurahat dekh ke mera sara ghum door ho jaata hai. Tumhari hansi sun ke lagta hai ke duniya mein koi melody isse zyada pyaari nahi. Tum mere liye sirf biwi nahi, tum meri sab se achi dost ho, meri partner ho, meri duniya ho. ✨

Har roz tum mere saath hoti ho toh lagta hai ke Allah ne mujhe duniya ki sabse badi naimat de di hai. Main tumhara shukar guzar hoon ke tum meri zindagi mein ho, ke tum ne mujhe itna pyaar diya, itna saath diya. 🫣

Happy Birthday meri jaan! Yeh saal tumhare liye sabse khoobsurat ho. Main waada karta hoon ke tumhara haath kabhi nahi chhodunga, tumhare saath har mushkil mein khadda rahunga, aur tumse har din pehle se zyada mohabbat karunga. 🌹

Tum sirf meri biwi nahi — tum meri rooh ho, meri dhadkan ho, meri puri kaynaat ho. Main Allah ka shuker ada karta hoon ke usne tumhe mere liye banaya. I love you more than words can ever say, Fiza! ❤️

Hamesha Tumhara,
Tumhara Basit ❤️
